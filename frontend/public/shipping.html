Shipping Policy

Thank you for visiting our website. Please read our Shipping Policy carefully.

This is a digital, entertainment-based service using publicly available insights. No private data is accessed.

1. Digital Product Delivery

We provide digital services only.

No physical items are shipped.

After successful payment:

  • Your service/report is delivered instantly on the website or email/SMS (depending on product type).
  • If delivery is delayed due to server load, you will still receive it within a few minutes.
  • There is no physical shipping involved.

2. Delivery Time

Digital delivery is usually instant.

In rare cases, it may take up to 30 minutes due to:

  • High server demand
  • Slow internet connectivity
  • Payment gateway verification delays

If you have not received your service within 30 minutes, please contact support.

3. Order Confirmation

Once payment is completed:

  • You will receive an order confirmation email/SMS
  • The service will be accessible through the website or provided directly

4. Incorrect Details

We are not responsible for delays or failed delivery if:

  • You enter an incorrect email
  • You enter an incorrect phone number
  • The device used cannot access the service

Please double-check your details before placing the order.

5. Support

For any issues regarding delivery, please contact us through our support channels.
