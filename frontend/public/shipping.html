Shipping Policy

Last updated: 26 December 2025

All orders are processed after successful payment confirmation.

Order processing typically begins within 24–48 hours of purchase. Orders are not processed or delivered on weekends or public holidays unless otherwise stated.

If we experience a high volume of orders, processing times may be slightly delayed. Customers will be notified in case of any significant delay.

Delivery timelines provided at checkout or on the website are estimates only and not guaranteed.

Orders are fulfilled using appropriate delivery methods as determined by the company. Delivery may be handled directly by us or through third-party service providers.

Once an order is processed and dispatched, responsibility for delivery timelines may depend on the selected method or service provider.

Shipping or handling charges, if applicable, are clearly displayed at checkout before payment is completed.

Any additional charges imposed by third-party providers, banks, or service partners are beyond our control.

Customers are responsible for providing accurate and complete information at the time of purchase.

  • Incorrect contact details

Orders affected by such issues may not be eligible for reshipment or compensation.

6. Delays & Issues

Delivery delays may occur due to factors beyond our control, including but not limited to:

  • Technical issues
  • Operational disruptions
  • Network or service provider delays
  • Regulatory or compliance checks

We are not liable for delays caused by such circumstances.

7. Lost or Undelivered Orders

If an order is marked as delivered but not received, or if delivery fails due to factors outside our control, customers must contact us within 24 hours of the expected delivery date for assistance.

Each case will be reviewed individually.

8. Changes to Orders

Once an order is placed and processing has begun, changes or cancellations may not be possible.

Customers are advised to review all details carefully before completing their purchase.

We reserve the right to modify or update this Shipping Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on the website.

Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For questions related to shipping or order status, customers should use the official contact method provided on the website.
