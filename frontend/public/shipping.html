Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: 28 December 2025

Creative Technologies provides digital services only. No physical goods are shipped.

Digital Service Delivery

All services purchased on the Platform are delivered electronically. Delivery is completed through one or more of the following methods:

  • Email confirmation
  • Online dashboard access
  • Digital reports, insights, or analytics access

Delivery Timeline

Digital services are delivered within 24 hours of successful payment, unless otherwise specified at the time of purchase.

In certain cases, delivery may take slightly longer due to technical verification or service processing requirements. Users will be informed accordingly.

Delivery Confirmation

Successful delivery of services will be confirmed via the registered email ID provided by the user at the time of purchase or registration.

No Physical Shipping

As all services are digital in nature:

  • No courier, postal, or logistics services are involved
  • No shipping charges apply
  • No physical address is required for delivery

Delays

While we strive to deliver services within the stated timeline, Creative Technologies shall not be liable for delays caused by:

  • Technical issues beyond our control
  • Incorrect information provided by the user
  • External platform limitations

In case of any delivery-related concerns, users are encouraged to contact our support team promptly.
