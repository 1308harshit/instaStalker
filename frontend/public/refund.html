Refund Policy

Refunds for services purchased on the Platform are governed by the following terms:

Refund requests will be considered only if the user has genuinely not received the purchased digital service. The request must be raised within 3 days from the date of successful payment. Each refund request is carefully reviewed to verify service non-delivery before approval.

Once a digital service has been successfully delivered, activated, accessed, or confirmed via the registered email ID of the user, no refund shall be applicable. Refunds will also not be provided in cases where the service outcome depends on user-provided information or external platform limitations beyond our control.

If a refund is approved after verification, the amount will be processed back to the original payment method within 2–5 business days, subject to the policies and processing timelines of the respective payment gateway or financial institution.

Any transaction charges, platform fees, or payment gateway charges, if applicable, are non-refundable.
