Refund & Cancellation Policy

Thank you for choosing our platform. We request you to carefully read this Refund & Cancellation Policy before making any purchase, as it governs all transactions made on our website.

This is a digital, entertainment-based service using publicly available insights. No private data is accessed.

Since the services are delivered immediately after successful payment, they are considered consumed at the time of delivery.

2. No Cancellation Policy

Once a payment has been successfully completed, cancellation of the order is not possible.

Users are advised to review the service details carefully before proceeding with payment.

3. No Refund Policy

Due to the digital and instant nature of the services, all purchases are final and non-refundable.

Refunds, reversals, or chargebacks are not permitted once the service has been delivered.

4. Limited Refund Exceptions

A refund may be considered only in rare and exceptional circumstances, subject to internal verification, including:

  • Successful payment made but no service delivered at all due to a confirmed technical error from our side.

In such cases, refund requests must be raised within 24 hours of the transaction.

5. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • User dissatisfaction or change of mind after purchase

  • Suspicion of misuse, abuse, or fraudulent activity

6. Chargebacks & Disputes

Initiating chargebacks or payment disputes without first contacting our support team may lead to:

  • Permanent suspension of service access
  • Blacklisting of the account and associated identifiers

We strongly encourage users to contact support for resolution before raising any dispute.

7. Support & Communication

For refund-related concerns (only applicable under Section 4), users must contact us with:

  • Transaction ID
  • Registered email address
  • Date and time of payment
  • Clear explanation of the issue

Our support team will review the request and respond within 3–5 business days.

8. Final Authority

All refund and cancellation decisions are made at the sole discretion of the company.

The company reserves the right to accept or reject any request without obligation to provide justification.

9. Policy Acceptance

By completing a purchase on our website, the user acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agreed to this Refund & Cancellation Policy in full.
