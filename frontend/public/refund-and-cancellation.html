Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 26 December 2025

This Refund & Cancellation Policy governs all purchases made on this website. By completing a transaction, you acknowledge and agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Order Confirmation

Once an order is successfully placed and payment is confirmed, the order is considered final and is immediately moved into processing.

Customers are advised to review all information carefully before completing their purchase.

2. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations are not permitted once an order has been placed and processing has begun.

We are unable to cancel, modify, or reverse orders after confirmation due to the nature of our order processing workflow.

3. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are not automatically guaranteed and are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

A refund may be considered only under the following circumstances:

  • The customer was charged more than once for the same transaction
  • A confirmed technical error occurred that prevented order fulfillment
  • Payment was successfully completed but the order was not processed due to a system issue on our end

Refunds will not be issued for reasons related to personal preference, change of mind, or misunderstanding after purchase.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Failure to read or understand the information presented before purchase
  • Dissatisfaction based on personal expectations
  • Customer errors, including incorrect details provided at checkout
  • Delays caused by external factors beyond our control
  • Failure to contact us within the stated timeframe

All completed transactions are considered final unless a valid exception applies.

5. Refund Request Timeframe

Any refund request must be submitted within 24 hours of the transaction date.

Requests received after this period will not be reviewed or approved.

6. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, customers must contact us through the official support channel listed on the website and provide:

  • Full name
  • Email address used during purchase
  • Transaction ID or payment reference
  • Date and time of purchase
  • Clear explanation of the issue

Incomplete or unclear requests may be rejected.

7. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method only.

Refund processing timelines:

  • Review: up to 5–7 business days
  • Bank or payment provider processing: 7–10 business days

Payment gateway fees, taxes, or processing charges may be deducted where applicable.

8. Chargebacks & Disputes

Customers are strongly encouraged to contact us first before initiating a chargeback or dispute with their bank or payment provider.

Unauthorized or abusive disputes may result in:

  • Immediate rejection of the refund request
  • Suspension of future access
  • Submission of transaction records and delivery proof to financial institutions

9. Abuse & Fraud Prevention

We reserve the right to deny refunds if we detect misuse, abuse, or fraudulent activity related to refund requests or disputes.

10. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated or modified at any time without prior notice. The version published on the website at the time of purchase will apply to that transaction.

11. Contact

All refund and cancellation inquiries must be submitted through the official contact details provided on the website.
