1. Nature of Products and Services

All products and/or services offered on this website are digital, online, instant-access, or non-physical services, unless explicitly mentioned otherwise on the respective product page.

Once access credentials, reports, dashboards, tools, digital files, or services are delivered or made accessible, the order is deemed successfully delivered and fulfilled.

Digital services, once delivered, cannot be returned.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are not automatic and are not guaranteed. All refund requests are reviewed strictly on a case-by-case basis.

A refund may be considered only under the following conditions:

  • The customer was charged more than once for the same transaction.
  • The customer did not receive access to the purchased service due to a verified technical failure on our end.
  • The delivered service is materially different from what was clearly described on the website at the time of purchase.

If none of the above conditions are met, the refund request will be rejected.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be issued under the following circumstances:

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Dissatisfaction due to personal expectations
  • Failure to achieve desired results or outcomes

  • Customer error, including incorrect details entered at checkout
  • Failure to use, access, or act upon the service after delivery
  • Claims made without verifiable proof
  • Requests raised after the allowed timeframe
  • Disputes or chargebacks raised directly with banks or payment gateways without first contacting us

All sales are final once access is delivered.

4. Refund Amount & Figures

If a refund request is approved:

  • The refund amount will be limited strictly to the amount paid by the customer.
  • This may include ₹99 (Rupees Ninety-Nine Only) or any other amount actually paid, depending on the transaction.
  • No refund will exceed the original payment amount under any circumstances.
  • No additional compensation, interest, damages, or penalties will be provided.

Example:
If a customer paid ₹99, the maximum refundable amount is ₹99 only.
If a customer paid ₹199, the maximum refundable amount is ₹199 only.

5. Refund Request Timeframe

All refund requests must be submitted within 24 hours of the original transaction time.

Refund requests submitted after 24 hours will be automatically rejected, regardless of reason.

6. Refund Request Procedure

To request a refund, the customer must contact the official support channel mentioned on the website and provide all of the following:

  • Full name

  • Order ID / Transaction ID
  • Exact date and time of purchase

Incomplete, vague, or unverifiable requests will be rejected without further communication.

7. Review & Processing Timeline

Refund requests are reviewed within 5–7 business days.

If approved, refunds are processed to the original payment method only.

Post-approval processing may take 7–10 business days, depending on the bank or payment provider.

Payment gateway fees, taxes, or processing charges may be deducted from the refunded amount where applicable.

Once an order is placed and access to the service is granted, cancellations are not permitted.

Customers are responsible for reviewing all product details, pricing, and descriptions before completing a purchase.

10. Fraud Prevention

Any attempt to abuse the refund system, provide false information, or manipulate transactions will result in:
