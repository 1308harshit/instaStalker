Cancellation Policy

Cancellations are permitted only if the request is made within 3 days of placing the order and before the digital service has been delivered, activated, or accessed.

Once a digital service has been initiated, processed, delivered, or access credentials / reports / insights have been shared with the user, cancellation requests will not be accepted.

Cancellation requests must be submitted through the official support channels provided on the Platform. Each request will be reviewed to verify the status of service delivery before approval.

If a cancellation is approved, any eligible refund will be processed in accordance with the Refund Policy and credited back to the original payment method within 2–5 business days, subject to payment gateway processing timelines.

The Platform Owner reserves the right to reject cancellation requests that do not meet the above conditions or where misuse or abuse of the policy is suspected.
